DESIGN NOTES

Resolution: 32×32 pixels native. SVG-based — every "pixel" is a <rect width="1" height="1"> so the glyph scales crisply from a 16-px map dot to a 512-px lore screen with image-rendering: pixelated.

Palette per faction: the Open Eye uses parchment + cyan + gold (matching the player colour throughout the HUD mockup). The Maw uses bone + blood-red + near-black (rival colour). Both share a parchment-and-ink sensibility so they feel like they belong in the same world, just to different gods.

Opposition: the Eye opens upward and outward; the Maw opens downward and inward. The Eye watches; the Maw consumes. Eye has rays; Maw has teeth. This is the visual rivalry — flip the page back and forth between the two and the gods declare themselves.

Game readability check: at 32 px the Eye should read as "round shape with a clear pupil and rays"; the Maw should read as "dark hollow with teeth". If you squint at the 32-px column above and either looks like a smear, that glyph needs revision.

What's next: if these work as logos, the next pass produces 2 more variants per faction (for relic slots 2 and 3), plus an explicit "shattered" treatment when a relic falls.
