DENSITAS  ·  HUD mockup v0.1

Annotated layout sketch for the prototype. Numbers refer to the legend below.
1
Population & tier — souls count and current tier name. Tier text glows when you cross a threshold.
2
Belief reservoir — global belief, regenerates from citizens; spent on casts. Bar pulses on regeneration.
3
The seam — drawn dashed where the two belief fields meet at parity. Citizens here are the contested ones; battles are won by pushing this line.
4
Mini-map — both density fields visible. The light box shows the camera viewport.
5
Scripture log — terse, reverent event log. Rival actions in their colour. Idle world emits the occasional "the wind carries no news" line.
6
Power tray — five tier slots. Unlocked tiers brighten; locked tiers grey out with their threshold visible. Hover a tier to see its powers.
